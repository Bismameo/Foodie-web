Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about foodie

Is there a minimum order value?

No, you can order as much or as little as you like! However, some restaurants may have their own minimum order requirements which will be shown at checkout.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll see a live tracking screen showing exactly where your food and rider are in real-time.

Can I pay with cash on delivery?

Yes! We accept Cash on Delivery, as well as all major Credit/Debit cards, digital wallets, and Apple/Google Pay.

How can I partner my restaurant with foodie?

We're always looking for new partners! Simply click on "Partner with Us" in the footer to start your application process.
